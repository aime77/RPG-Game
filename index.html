RPG Superheroes Game

INSTRUCTIONS

Choose your favorite hero and then your least favorite hero to fight against.

Attack your oponents by pressing the "Attack" button.

If you defeat your oponent, select another oponent to attack.

You won when you're the last hero standing.

SOUND: If you want to add sentiment to your experience, hover over the left bottom area of the screen. You will find a audio control to play awesome music for heroes!
